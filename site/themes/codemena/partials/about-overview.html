Our Story

After several years of running startups and suffering from poorly-designed products, we set out to solve the problem that plagued not just us, but hundreds of startups across the region.

We traveled around the world studying the practices of elite tech startups and renouned software agencies attending events and workshops across Europe and North America.

The result is Code MENA, a boutique design and development agency that merges the quality, culture, and workflows of the most cutting-edge companies around the world, while bringing an understanding of the MENA region and the average Arab consumer.

Code MENA was founded in early 2018 with one goal: help startups build amazing products that their users will love using.

Our agency proudly holds its headquarters in Amman, Jordan with remote branches in the United States and United Kingdoms.
